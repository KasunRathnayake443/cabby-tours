Founded in Colombo over a decade ago, Cabby Tours was born from a simple belief — that the best way to experience Sri Lanka is through the eyes of someone who truly loves it.

We are a team of passionate local experts, drivers, and storytellers who know every mountain trail, every hidden beach, and every family-run restaurant worth stopping at. We don't just take you to the destinations — we take you into the heart of the island.
